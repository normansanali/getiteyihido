    Top Facts about Jeff Probst

  • Jeff Probst was born on November 4, 1961 in Wichita, Kansas.
  • He graduated from Newport High School in 1979.
  • He is best known as the Emmy Award-winning host of the U.S. version of the reality television show Survivor since 2000.
  • He was a frequent guest star on the sketch show MADtv.
  • He is the host of Survivor, a globally-syndicated American reality show which he has hosted since its inception in 2000.
  • People magazine named Probst one of the “50 Most Beautiful People” in 2001.
  • In 2004, he began dating one of the contestants from Survivor: Vanuatu, Julie Berry.
  • He wrote and directed the Lionsgate released film, Finder’s Fee.
  • Probst made a guest appearance in a 2003 episode of Space Ghost Coast to Coast.
  • He hosted “Celebrity Superfan Roundtable” for Howard Stern.
  • He hosted the syndicated daytime talk show The Jeff Probst Show from September 2012 to May 2013.
  • He was married to his first wife, psychotherapist Shelley Wright, from 1996 to 2001.
  • He married his second spouse, Lisa Ann Russell, on December 5, 2011.
  • He is the author of a book called Stranded, based on the show Survivor.
  • He has won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Host for a Reality or Reality-Competition Program four consecutive times from 2008 to 2011.
