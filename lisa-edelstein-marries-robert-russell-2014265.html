    26 Aug 2024, 00:00

    'Twas the weekend of love! The day after Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's massive European wedding extravaganza, House star Lisa Edelstein tied the knot with artist Robert Russell on Sunday, May 25, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

    Edelstein, 48, is best known for her seven-season run as Dr. Lisa Cuddy on the medical drama. She surprisingly left the Fox show in 2011, going on to guest star on hit shows like The Good Wife, Elementary, Scandal, House of Lies, and Castle.
