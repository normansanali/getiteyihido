    Opposite day! After steaming things up in next to nothing in paradise (aka St. Bart’s) with Harry Styles over the New Year, Kendall Jenner got back to reality by covering way up on Sunday, January 3.

    The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 20, grabbed a cup of joe in L.A., layering basic black running tights and a taupe turtleneck under a statement white fur coat by Express. She kept her vibe low-key with a pair of aviators and black and gray Nike tennis shoes.

    This back-to-real-life look comes as a major contrast to the tiny red bikini Jenner sported while canoodling with the One Direction band member, 21, on December 29 and 31. 

    A source tells Us Weekly that Styles hoped to rekindle their 2013 romance after seeing Jenner work her curves at the 2015 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show. “Her appearance in the fashion show is what drew him back to her,” the source explains. “All of his friends were saying how hot and amazing she looked.”
