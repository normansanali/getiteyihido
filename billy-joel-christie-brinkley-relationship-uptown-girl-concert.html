    Before "Uptown Girl" was released in 1983, Joel ultimately wrote the song about Brinkley. During an interview with Howard Stern that was quoted by Far Out Magazine, Joel told the story about why he wrote the song.

    As Joel explained, he began writing the original version of the song when he was dating Elle McPherson.

    “What they were trying to do was debunk that it was all about Christie. I wasn’t even dating Christie when I started writing the song; I was dating Elle. It was an amazing time. That’s why I started writing a song called ‘Uptown Girls’. It was plural. I couldn’t believe the situation I was in; it was unbelievable.”

    However, as Joel explained to Stern, the song went through a huge change after he broke up with McPherson. Following Joel and McPherson's split, he went on to begin dating Christie Brinkley. As Joel told Stern, the version of "Uptown Girl" that was released was written about Brinkley.

    “Then I started to date Christie, after Elle and I had stopped dating. Rather than the song being about all these different girls, she became the uptown girl. I started writing it about one person. They got the story wrong. It is about Christie.”
