    Khloe Kardashian is trying hard to make her relationship with Tristan Thompson work – even if they are living completely separate lives. Thompson is in the middle of another NBA season, and his work requires him to travel across the country on a weekly basis.

    This week, for example, Thompson was in New Orleans for a game against the Pelicans.

    While Thompson was warming up with his Cleveland Cavaliers teammates, Kardashian was busy on the set of Keeping Up With the Kardashians in Los Angeles.
