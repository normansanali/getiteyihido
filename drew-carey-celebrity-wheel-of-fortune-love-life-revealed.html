    As an entertainer, Drew Carey has had quite the career. The ex-marine turned comedian has hosted a number of hit shows over the years including Whose Line is it Anyway?,  The Price is Right and his own talk show, The Drew Carey Show.

    But how much do you know about the famed comedian when the cameras are off? Find out more about Drew Care's love life here ...

    Drew is appearing on Celebrity Wheel of Fortune

    Engagement to Nicole Jaracz 

    In the same year that Drew replaced Bob Barker as the host of The Price is Right, he got engaged for the first time to chef Nicole Jaracz. While details about the couple’s relationship remain undisclosed, the two reportedly met in the mid-2000s.  

    Drew proposed to girlfriend Nicole in 2007

    However, the two never made it down the aisle as Drew and Nicole amicably ended their engagement in 2012. Drew’s representatives told People at the time: “He and Nicole still have a great deal of love and affection for one another,” adding: “He will still be very involved with their son’s life.”

    Drew remains close to both Nicole and her son from a previous relationship, Connor even today. In fact, he even credits Connor, who Drew refers to as his own son with inspiring his life-changing weight loss.

    “I couldn’t keep up with him,” the TV personality explained to Parade. “I’d be like, ‘Connor, I can’t,’ and he’d say, ‘C’mon, Dad!’ That was a terrible feeling. I thought, I’m never going to see him graduate high school.” Thanks to Connor, Drew shed over 100 pounds. 

    Engagement to Amie Harwick

    Six years after ending his relationship with Nicole, Drew popped the question again, this time to licensed marriage and family therapist, Amie Harwick. The pair reportedly hit it off after meeting at a party in 2017 and got engaged a few months later.

    The couple regularly stepped out on the red carpet together and piled on the PDA on social media but sadly it wasn’t meant to be and the couple ended their engagement in November 2018.

    Drew became engaged for a second time in 2017 to girlfriend Amie

    The two remained close friends up until Amie’s death in 2020. The 38-year-old was found at her Hollywood Hills home after being attacked by ex-boyfriend Gareth Pursehouse in February of last year.

    When Drew learnt of the news of Amie’s tragic death he withdrew from filming for The Price is Right for a week and took to Twitter to pay tribute to his late ex-fiance. He shared a photo of the two alongside the message: “Hope you’re lucky enough to have someone in your life that loves as much as she did.”

    "Amie and I had a love that people are lucky to have once in a lifetime," Carey said in a statement to CNN. "She was a positive force in the world, a tireless and unapologetic champion for women, and passionate about her work as a therapist. I am overcome with grief. I would like to thank you in advance for giving myself and everyone who loved Amie privacy while we try to work through this tragic situation."
