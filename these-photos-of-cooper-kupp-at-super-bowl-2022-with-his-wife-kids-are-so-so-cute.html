    Cooper Kupp was the MVP of the 2022 Super Bowl and his whole family was there to celebrate his big victory!

    The 28-year-old football player, who is the wide receiver for the Los Angeles Rams, won the big game on Sunday night (February 13) at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles.

    Following the victory, Cooper was joined on the field by his wife Anna Marie and their two kids – Cooper Jr. and Cypress!

    “I just don’t have words,” Kupp said in response to being named MVP (via ESPN). “I’m just so thankful for everyone.”

    Kupp helped bring the team to a victory by having four catches for 39 yards on the final drive. He also had the game-winning score.
