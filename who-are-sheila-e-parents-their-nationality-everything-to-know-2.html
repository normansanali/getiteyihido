    Sheila E’s interest in music arose from her parents’ support and inspiration. So, who are her proud parents?

    Sheila E is an American percussionist.

    The 63-year-old performer has performed with famed stars like Marvin Gaye, Herbie Hancock, Mariah Carey, and Stevie Wonder, and many other celebrities.

    Who Are Sheila E Parents?

    Sheila E’s parents’ names are Juanita Gardere and Pete Escovedo as noted in her Wikipedia.

    Her father was involved as a renowned Latin and jazz percussionist whereas her mother Juanita had an engagement in a dairy factory as a worker. Furthermore, her parents full-named her Sheila Cecilia Escovedo, but later, she was known in the world by her stage name Sheila E.

    The 1957 born artist Sheila was born into a musical family as per All Music. Thanks to that, she and her younger brothers were inspired by music and instruments from a young age.

    Sheila E Ethnicity And Nationality Explored

    Sheila E is mixed by ethnicity thanks to her parents’ origin.

    From the online source, we got to learn that her mother has descendants from Creole-France or Africa. Similarly, her father Pete is of Mexican-American descent.

    Given her place of birth in Oakland, California, obviously, Sheila E holds an American nationality. The artist also has the right to enjoy other citizenship belonging to either her dad’s side or mother’s.

    As per the said web portal, the drummer has a collection of around $12 million in net worth.

    Who Is Sheila E Married To? Does She Have A Child?

    As far as we know, Sheila E is not married to anyone till now, but, she had a fiance as per Hollywood Reporter.
