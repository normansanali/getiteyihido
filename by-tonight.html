    Overall Meaning

    NoCap is a rapper from Mobile, Alabama who accrued widespread prominence with his 2018 mixtape, "Neighborhood Hero." In "by tonight," NoCap talks about deep-seated subjects like drugs and crime that are plaguing his life. He goes on about listening to his gangster inner voice to get by, maintaining his grip on truth in a world where lies and deceptions pull individuals down. In the first verse, NoCap communicates how he has demons and guns in his life, and he's attempting to make it out alive, facing hardships head-on. He mentions that he can tell someone has been crying, and he feels guilty for splurging on a designer purchase that didn't fix anything, a clever reference to the fact that money can't buy happiness. In the chorus, the artist sings about having guns in his hand and troops on the front line while making references to Suge Knight, while indicating that his turbulent lifestyle has led him down an obsessive path of expensive watches and having to duck for authorities.


    In the second verse, NoCap speaks about drug addiction, how he'd spotted a knot on his head, purportedly from an accidental hard hit when he needed money to fuel his dependence. He then referenced drug usage and how it's often present in low-income areas, including his neighbourhood. He then discusses his doubts about a female, unsure if she's loyal and deceiving him. Finally, the rapper talks about how he typically plays it low-key but is still focused on fame, and his persevering faith that everything will turn out alright.
