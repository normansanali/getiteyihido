    Kim Kardashian Attends High School Reunion With Old Friends

    Kim Kardashian has had a super busy week, especially following the birth of sister Khlo Kardashian's baby girl. But she still found time to attend her 20 year high school reunion, and it'll give you major nostalgia.

    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kim Kardashian has had a super busy week, especially following the birth of sister Khloé Kardashian's baby girl. But she still found time to attend her 20 year high school reunion, and it'll give you major nostalgia.

    Related Story

    Taking to Snapchat, and her Instagram Story, on Friday night, Kim shared photos and clips of her high school friends. To make sure that the evening was as legendary as possible, Kim rented a party bus to take the group back to their high school to hang out with the rest of the Marymount alumni. And, yes, there were copious "Class of '98" balloons:

    Instagram

    Talking to camera, Kim revealed her fears about attending her high school reunion, saying, "So my friends are worried that we're going to run into people that they were mean to... I feel so good about my conscience, I was so nice to everyone."

    As well as attending with her group of high school besties, Kim posted for photos with her former classmates, who all looked elated to be catching up with the makeup mogul:

    Instagram

    Kim also revealed that she attended an all-girls Catholic school, which explains why there were no men at the reunion, like, at all:

    Instagram

    The reality star also took the opportunity to leaf through some high school yearbooks, and presumably revisit the hopes, dreams, and hairstyles she had as a teenager:

    Snapchat

    Feeling nostalgic about her return to high school, Kim explained, "We are walking the same halls we walked 20 years ago..." She also opened up about the one class she struggled with, saying, "This is where chemistry was back in the day—this is where I did really badly in this chemistry class," which is more than a little relatable.
