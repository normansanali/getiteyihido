    About

    Punk and heavy metal bassist and songwriter who became known for his work with the California band Suicidal Tendencies. He also performed with Los Cycos, Horny Toad, and Luicidal.

    Before Fame

    He wrote such early Suicidal Tendencies hits as "War Inside My Head" and "Institutionalized."

    His Suicidal Tendencies albums include Welcome to Venice, which was released in 1985, and Join the Army, which was released in 1987.
