    Musician RAC Parents

    Musician RAC was born to his parents Armenio Anjos and Elizabeth Anjos in Coimbra, Portugal. His parents reportedly met each other when his father moved to the US when his mom was 19 years old. A year later they got married and moved back to Portugal when she was 20 years old. Then a year later after she turned 21, RAC was born.

    His parents were missionaries, so he always felt to go against the grain. His mother raised him and his brother on a very modest salary but made sure to make the ends meet. They struggled financially but his parents never let them have a sense of it.

    At the age of 15, Anjos’ mother delivered a box of cereal that would forever alter his life: Anjos discovered a CD containing a demo of recording software at the bottom of the bag of sugary puffs, where one could typically find some sort of action figure or toy. This demo was essentially a simple, outdated version of Garageband for PCs. His creative world was expanded by it.

    Anjos works at International Sports Ministries. He studied Sports Chaplaincy at Baylor University, Theology and Psychology at Liberty University, and also attended Word of Life Bible Institute and Instituto Bíblico Português. Armenio is currently residing in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania.

    His wife Elizabeth runs a jewelry and watch shop.
