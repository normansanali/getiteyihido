    Devin Bartolotta Biography and Wiki

    Devin Bartolotta is an American three-time Emmy-winning anchor and reporter who works for WWL-TV since September 2019. Previously, before joining WWL-TV, she spent three years as both anchor and reporter at WJZ-TV in Baltimore, Maryland. 

    As a renowned anchor and an acclaimed reporter, Bartolotta covered the rapid aftermath of the Capital Gazette newsroom shooting, historic and fatal flooding in Ellicott City, along with the record-setting killing rates in Baltimore City. In 2017, together with the WJZ team, got nominated for an Emmy Award for breaking news coverage of an officer who died in the line of duty.

    She started her broadcast career at t KTTC-TV in Rochester, Minnesota, where she served as a producer, editor, and as a weekend anchor. While in Rochester, Devin was recognized multiple times by the Associated Press and Society of Professional Journalists.
