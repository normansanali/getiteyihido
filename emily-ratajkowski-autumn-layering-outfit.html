    Emily Ratajkowski is ready for autumn.

    The model and My Body author stepped out yesterday in New York City's West Village, wearing a cozy, sporty outfit. EmRata practiced some unexpected layering with her look, which was composed of an oversize blue crewneck sweatshirt paired with a brown, distressed leather jacket, and gray skinny jeans.

    (Similar jackets are available at Zadig & Voltaire and Asos, and similar sweatshirts at Ssense and Halfdays.)

    For footwear, she opted for white-and-gray New Balance sneakers worn with fuzzy white-and-purple socks. Ratajkowski further accessorized with rectangular brown tortoiseshell sunglasses and large gold hoop earrings. She let her straight hair down and seemed to wear minimal makeup.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Ratajkowski was photographed walking with her friends in the fit.

    Her dive into fall dressing comes after she stepped out last week in a semi-sheer vintage Jean Paul Gaultier halter dress. She wore the piece to the 2023 MTV VMAs. The handkerchief dress included a crisscrossing halter neck and abdominal straps in a vibrant emerald green; its asymmetrical skirt featured a rusty orange surrealist illustration of a woman diving in circles.

    Ratajkowski wore another dress—a black knit mini—over the weekend. (It was the same decronstructed minidress that Kylie Jenner once posed in on her Instagram Story.) The piece—which hails from Acne Studios’ Fall 2023 collection—features loose-knit strands, distressed cutouts, floral knit embroidery, and tendrils of yarn wrapped around the neck and hanging off the shoulders. The High Low podcast host accessorized with a black leather half-moon bag from Prada and a pair of black pumps with matching knit leg warmers.
