    Is there any reason to keep gems in Skyrim?

    Some gems are important for smithing jewelry, which gives lots of experience for both smithing and enchanting. Thus, it is recommended to save all gems used in jewelry crafting recipes.

    Should I sell or keep gems in Skyrim?

    I usually hang onto gems and smith them into jewelry, then sell or enchant the result. All general goods vendors should buy them, and you can also take speech perks to invest in shops for better prices.

    Can you sell amethyst in Skyrim?

    You should be able to sell amethysts at general goods stores such as the Riverwood Trader or Belethor’s General Goods Store in Whiterun.

    What should I do with gems in Skyrim?

    Gems are raw materials for crafting jewelry and can be sold for a considerable amount of gold. They can also be used to create Void Salts at the Atronach Forge.

    What is the most useful stone in Skyrim?

    The Lover Stone is widely considered the best Standing Stone in the game. It grants the Lover’s Comfort effect all the time, which increases the rate at which the player’s skills improve.

    What is the use of amethyst in Skyrim?

    Amethysts have exceptional value and light weight, making them a very good source of income. They can also be used in the crafting of jewelry and are seldom used in quests. Amethysts can also be used to create Void Salts at the Atronach Forge.

    How much should I sell my amethyst for?

    The price of an amethyst can range anywhere between $20 to $50 per carat, depending on various factors such as color and clarity.

    What is the most valuable jewelry in Skyrim?

    Who needs flawless amethyst in Skyrim?

    Talen-Jei will ask you to find three flawless amethysts for a ring so he can marry Keevara. Deliver him the amethysts and you will receive a potion of minor stamina as a reward.

    What should I not sell in Skyrim?

    Can my house be robbed in Skyrim?

    No, your house cannot be robbed in Skyrim. Chests cannot be overloaded with items, so your belongings are safe.

    What happens if you keep touching the earth stone in Skyrim?

    If you touch the Earth Stone in Skyrim, many inhabitants of Raven Rock will have been indoctrinated by Miraak and are forced to construct a shrine around it. Touching the stone at this point will enslave your character as well, though any movement or input will break Miraak’s hold.
