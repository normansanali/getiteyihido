    Best MTZ 556 Loadout and Class Setup in MW3

    Best MTZ 556 Loadout in Modern Warfare 3 Pre-Season

    The MTZ 556 assault rifle in Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3 works more like an SMG. Thanks to its extremely fast fire rate, it can burst through enemies extremely quickly. Thanks to its exceptional performance both at close and mid-range, it is a very versatile weapon which can serve you well in various situations and encounters.
