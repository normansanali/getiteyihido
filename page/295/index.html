What does it mean when cauliflower is fuzzy?

19 Jul 2024, 00:00

When a cauliflower head matures very rapidly, a condition called ricing can occur. Ricing is a condition in which the curd develops small flower buds that appear velvety or fuzzy. Optimally, the cauliflower head is harvested before that condition occurs. Can you eat cauliflower that is fuzzy? The cauliflower is not adversely affected, except by the appearance. The defect has no bearing on the taste of the cauliflower. Overmature cauliflower, or cauliflower exposed to extreme heat while growing may taste somewhat bitter, but don't assume all cauliflower with fuzzy heads will taste bitter.
