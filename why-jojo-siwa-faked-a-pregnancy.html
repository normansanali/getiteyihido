    Highlights

    • JoJo Siwa faced criticism for joking about pregnancy despite previously denying the rumors. Some felt it was insensitive and hurtful.

    The world was first introduced to JoJo Siwa in 2015 when she joined the fifth season of Dance Moms. Siwa had charisma that made her the most successful cast member on Dance Moms. Unlike some of the girls on the reality show, Siwa was not afraid to speak her mind when it came to dance coach, Abby Lee Miller.

    Over the years, Siwa was applauded by fans for speaking her mind and being herself. Be it coming out as gay in 2021, calling out Nickelodeon after they did not invite her to the Kids' Choice Awards in 2022, or defending the LGBTQIA community in 2022 when Candace Cameron Bure stated the reason she moved from Hallmark to Great American Family was to make "traditional marriage" movies, Siwa's fans rallied around her.

    In 2023, Siwa pretended to be pregnant, but her fans defended her because they felt there was no malice in Siwa's harmless joke. However, others did not feel the same and before too long, the entire situation got out of hand, though fans still think JoJo didn't do anything wrong.

    How Pregnancy Rumors With JoJo Siwa Started

    JoJo Siwa led fans to believe she was pregnant on Snapchat

    In early March 2023, Siwa posted a Snapchat. In that now-deleted snap, Siwa was holding her stomach which appeared to have a pooch. Given that Siwa had just broken up with her girlfriend, Avery Cyrus in December 2022, fans were confused by what they were seeing.

    At the time, it was rumored that Siwa had a secret girlfriend. Fans wondered if the pregnancy could be true and took screengrabs of the Snapchat in which Siwa had on-screen captions that read, "Can't believe it" and "Baby clothes shopping is my new favorite thing." Needless to say, the pictures went viral nearly immediately.

    Some fans expressed their concern with Siwa being pregnant given that she was still a teenager. Others stated the post was nothing more than Siwa with a "food baby" after having eaten a large meal. Fans were unable to agree on what was happening.

    Because the rumors of Siwa would not die down, according to Business Insider, Siwa took to TikTok to state she was not pregnant. This seemed to be the end of the pregnancy rumors and fans seemed relieved.

    However, at the end of March, the "pregnancy" pictures again began to circulate. This time, a screengrab from Snapchat showed an on-screen caption that stated, "TEAM BOY OR TEAM GIRL."

    Siwa returned to social media to make fun of the viral posts claiming she was pregnant. This time, according to Business Insider, Siwa and her friend Ezra Sosa filmed a Snapchat with Siwa holding her sweatshirt up. Sosa can be heard saying, "You know everyone thinks I'm the dad, right?"

    The on-screen caption read, "The father of my baby!!"

    Shortly thereafter, Siwa posted yet another Snapchat that showed her shopping for baby clothes. Needless to say, fans were perplexed with what to believe.

    It was later revealed that Siwa was not pregnant but was buying clothes for pregnant fans who may need them.

    While this should have been seen as a positive thing that Siwa was doing, she was instead criticized for pretending to be pregnant, which was confusing given that Siwa tried to dispel the pregnancy rumors just weeks before.

    Why JoJo Siwa Was Criticized For The Pregnancy Rumors

    Critics didn't like that JoJo joked about pregnancy

    Even though Siwa tried to shut down the pregnancy rumors at the beginning of March 2023, the same pictures and more circulated later that month. Given that the first time Siwa made a video about not being pregnant it was essentially ignored, she did what she thought was best and that was to make fun of the situation. In making light, however, Siwa came under fire by many. But it should be noted that none of the people calling Siwa out were her devoted fans.

    "It’s rude and hurtful to pretend to be pregnant when there is so many individuals struggling with fertility," one Reddit user wrote. "Perhaps it’s just a young person not putting the two together, but this is exactly why it’s not cool faking pregnancies for April Fools."

    A second user said, "Personally I find it pretty distasteful to fake things like pregnancy."

    "Basically, joke or not, it’s sending the wrong message," a third Reddit user stated. "...I feel like she should see what she’s doing is wrong. Especially when pregnancy is such a sensitive topic for a lot of people."

    While many were upset with Siwa for what she did, her fans continuously tried to point out that it was all meant to be a joke. Some even pointed out that while it might be "clickbait," Siwa was not trying to make light of women who were unable to get pregnant. However, the controversy continued for weeks, until it finally died down in April 2023.

    Though being criticized for a joke is never fun, there is something good that came out of the situation: Siwa donating a large amount of baby supplies to fans in need.

    The Positive That Came From The JoJo Siwa Pregnancy Rumors

    JoJo Siwa reportedly donated baby goods to people in need

    As Siwa was shopping for baby clothes and supplies, it was revealed that the shopping spree was not for her. Instead, all things baby were going to be donated to Siwa's fans. However, it was not Siwa that broke this news to fans.

    Some X (formerly Twitter) users informed social media just what Siwa's intentions were with the baby supplies. They were also part of the group that explained just why Siwa should be praised for what she was doing and not chastised.

    X user @siwa_gay broke the news that Siwa was buying new baby clothes "for giving away to mothers in need."

    "Say what you want about JoJo Siwa," a second X user wrote, "but her buying brand new baby clothes to send to expecting mothers and newborn babies' families is so pure."

    While it was these users who broke the news that Siwa was giving away baby supplies, it has never been disclosed whether Siwa donated said supplies. Nor has it been disclosed just what Siwa was trying to donate.

    However, fans are convinced that Siwa is a woman of her word. They have not found evidence to show that Siwa did not donate and because of this, Siwa's fans are standing by their stories that Siwa is generous and was just trying to help mothers in need.
