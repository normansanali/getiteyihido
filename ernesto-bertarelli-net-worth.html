Bertarelli’s journey towards success began with his education at Babson College, where he graduated in 1989. He further honed his skills with an MBA from Harvard Business School in 1993.

    “Education is the foundation on which I built my career. It provided me with the tools necessary to navigate the complex world of business and helped shape my entrepreneurial mindset,” said Bertarelli.

    After completing his education, Bertarelli took on the role of CEO at Serono, a pharmaceutical company, in 1996. In this position, he played a pivotal role in steering the company towards a biotechnology-focused approach, leading to substantial growth and increased revenues. His visionary leadership helped Serono’s revenues soar from $809 million in 1996 to $2.8 billion in 2006.

    Career of Ernesto Bertarelli

    Ernesto Bertarelli’s career is marked by his involvement in various business ventures, including his notable role at Serono. Additionally, Bertarelli has been involved with Ares Life Sciences, which focuses on investing in pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

    Sailing Achievements of Ernesto Bertarelli

    Ernesto Bertarelli, in addition to his successful business career, has made significant accomplishments in the world of sailing.
