    Hey there! Let’s have a share news about Eddie Levert, a name you’ve probably heard if you’re into soulful music and rich voices. Recently, the internet was buzzing with a rather alarming question: “Is Eddie Levert still alive?” Well, I’m here to set the record straight and tell you more about this legendary musician. Stick around, and let’s dive into the life of Eddie Levert, covering his bio, age, wife, net worth, kids, and more. It’s like we’re sharing stories about an old friend!

    Is Eddie Levert Still Alive?

    So, first things first: Is Eddie Levert still kicking? Absolutely, he is! The internet can be a wild place, you know, and in 2021 some bogus news started spreading like wildfire, claiming Eddie Levert had passed away. But hey, don’t believe everything you read online. After a bit of digging, it turns out that Eddie Levert is very much alive and well. I mean, imagine the surprise of his fans! It’s a relief to know that this was just a baseless rumor.

    Eddie Levert Bio

    Eddie Levert, or Edward Wills Levert, is a name that resonates with generations of music lovers. Born on June 16, 1942, in Bessemer, Alabama, Eddie is 79 years young as of 2022. He’s best known as the lead singer of the O’Jays, and what a journey it’s been since he started his career in 1958!

    Growing up, Eddie moved to Canton, Ohio, with his family and went to McKinley High School. His love for music blossomed early, and he teamed up with his childhood buddy Walter Williams to start singing. Inspired by Frankie Lyman and the Teenagers, they formed their first band, The Triumphs, with other students. Talk about humble beginnings, right?

    Eddie Levert Wife

    Eddie’s personal life is just as interesting. He’s married to Raquel, and together they have a daughter named Ryan. They’re currently living the good life in Las Vegas. Fun fact: NBA player Caris LeVert is Eddie’s third cousin. Life threw some tough times at Eddie, though. His son Gerald passed away in 2006 due to complications from prescribed medications. Heartbreakingly, his son Sean also passed away in 2008 under tragic circumstances. Eddie honors their memory through his music and continues to inspire many.

    Eddie Levert’s Net Worth

    Now, let’s talk numbers. Eddie Levert has made quite the fortune with his talent. As of now, his net worth stands at a cool $18 million. Not bad, right? From being the lead singer of the O’Jays to his solo career, Eddie has truly made a mark in the music industry.

    Eddie Levert Kids

    Eddie’s legacy extends through his sons, Gerald (1966–2006) and Sean (1968–2008). They followed in their dad’s footsteps and formed the band LeVert. You might remember their hit “Baby Hold On to Me,” which Eddie and Gerald recorded together. It’s a touching reminder of their bond.

    Throughout his career, Eddie has seen his fair share of accolades. He and Gerald won the “Best Duo or Group” Image Award in 2008, and the O’Jays received BET’s “Lifetime Achievement Award” in 2009, among other honors. The O’Jays have released numerous gold and platinum albums, and Eddie still tours and performs, both with the group and as a solo artist.
