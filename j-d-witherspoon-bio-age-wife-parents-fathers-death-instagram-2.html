    J.D Witherspoon Biography

    J.D Witherspoon is an American Youtube celebrity, actor, comedian and gamer who is mostly known for his WhoisUTV and runJDrun channels.

    Witherspoon is also a skilled impressionist and a rapper as well. He has shared some impressions of Drake and the cast of Family Guy.

    He has worked on The Equalizer 2, Roman J. Israel Esq. and does voice-over work for animated shows on Adult Swim such as Lazor Wulf and The Jellies.

    J.D Witherspoon Age

    Witherspoon was born on  June 23, 1988, in California.

    J.D Witherspoon Wife

    J.D is a married man. He is married to his wife who is known as pilarteaspoon on Instagram. The couple got married in 2017.

    J.D Witherspoon Parents

    J.D is the son of actor and comedian John Witherspoon and actress and director Angela Robinson Witherspoon.

    His father was a comedian and actor who happened to appear in so many TV shows and films. He is actually best known for his role as Willie Jones for the Friday series.

    Besides that, he also starred in films such as Hollywood Shuffle (1987), Boomerang (1992) and Vampire In Brooklyn (1995).

    His father died at his home on October 29, 2019. He was 77 years old at the time of his death.

    On the other side, his mother, Angela, has appeared on films and portrayed characters like caretaker Cassie Campbell in the Season Twelve episodes “Surface Tension”, “A Good Husband”, and “Green Light.”

    He has a younger brother named Alexander Witherspoon.

    J.D Witherspoon Father Death

    J.D’s father, John Witherspoon died on October 29, 2019, at his home, at the age of 77. J.D took to Instagram to eulogize his father writing; “So…my Dad died today & honestly I’m not sure how to feel. I’m sad, but I’m also happy 4 all the great times we had together.

    “We’d roast each other like homies more than Father & Son, and I really loved that about us. In all seriousness, I work as hard as I do thanks to seeing Him grind for literally 77 years of his life, and I’m damn proud to be his son.

    “If I have even half the career & impact he has had on people I’ll be very happy. His legacy will live on through me, my family, his friends, and everyone else who was touched by him here on earth.
    He was my best friend & my Hero.
    I Love U Dad…I’ll miss u. – J.D”
